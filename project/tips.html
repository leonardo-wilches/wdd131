Cake Decoration Tips

1. Combining Colors Effectively

It's important to consider the colors of your cake when choosing the color of your cake topper; this will influence the overall design and style.

2. Choosing the Right Cake Board

When choosing your cake board, remember to leave enough margin for your cake. For example, if you have a cake with a 20cm diameter, you should consider a 5cm margin each side, meaning the appropriate base should be 30cm.

3. Placing Your Cake Topper

This seems very simple, but mistakes are always made. The cake topper stick shouldn't be visible; it should be fully inserted into the cake so the topper stands out. If you have decorative elements between the letters and the cake surface, then you should take advantage of the height the cake topper stick provides.

4. Paper Butterflies

When using paper decorations, remember that the colors should coordinate and follow the style of your cake. Place them lightly on the surface for a more natural look.

5. Guide Bases

If you need to make two-tiered or very tall cakes, we recommend using cake boards with holes to give your cake stability. You should subtract 1 cm from each side of the cake board compared to the size of your cake pan; that is, if your cake is 20 cm, the cake board should be 18 cm.
