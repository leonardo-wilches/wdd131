About Deco Toppers

Deco Toppers was born out of the need many bakers had to bring their cakes to life with sturdiness and style. We use different materials and technologies to manufacture our products, such as 3D printing and laser cutting. Our brand gradually gained recognition, and today we have clients throughout the country.

Each product is made with love and designed to make your cakes stand out on every occasion!
